Join us at the CSN Cafe

Each of the CSN campuses, including Charleston, Henderson, and North Las Vegas, features a vibrant CSN Cafe where students and faculty can enjoy a variety of meals and snacks. Whether you're grabbing a quick coffee before class or sitting down for a meal, our cafes are designed to cater to your needs.

Catering Options

Exciting news for event planners and party throwers: Catering on campus is expertly handled by Chartwells, who collaborate closely with CSN Events to deliver exceptional food and service for your special occasions. Visit our catering section to explore the options available!

For a personalized touch to your events, CSN Catering offers bespoke services. If you have special requests or need advice, please don't hesitate to reach out to us. Our catering guide provides detailed information, but we are more than happy to create unique arrangements tailored to your needs.

To place a catering order, please contact Miguel Calderon at least one week in advance to meet safety and inventory requirements. We strive to ensure everything is perfectly aligned with your expectations for a smooth and successful event.
